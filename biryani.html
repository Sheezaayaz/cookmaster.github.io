Biryani is a mixed rice dish most popular in South Asia and was thought to have originated from ancient Iran. It is made with rice, some type of meat and spices.
To cater to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables for the meat. Sometimes eggs and/or potatoes are added Mutton; Chicken; Beef; Eggs Potatoes
Chicken Biryani, Mutton Biryani and Veg Biryani are some of the variants of this delicious dish. This dish is my favourite because it has a lip-smacking flavour, and I love to eat it at least once a week.
